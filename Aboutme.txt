### Tips to make your portfolio stand out

Recruiters in AI and Data Science often spend only a minute or two on a portfolio. Focus on quality over quantity by showcasing 3 to 5 well-documented projects. For each project, include:

* The problem you solved
* The dataset used
* The approach or algorithms
* The tools and technologies
* Results and evaluation metrics
* A GitHub repository with clean, well-commented code
* Screenshots or visualizations if applicable

A polished portfolio with a few strong projects is usually much more effective than one with many incomplete or poorly documented projects.
